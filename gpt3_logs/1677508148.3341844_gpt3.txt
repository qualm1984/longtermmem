- The four main components of a computer system are hardware, software, data, and people.
- Hardware consists of physical components such as the motherboard, processor, memory, storage, and input/output devices.
- Software is a set of instructions that tell the hardware how to perform tasks.
- Data is the information that is processed by the computer.
- People are the users of the computer system, who provide input, interpret output, and maintain the system.
